# Phoenix Air — Airline Policies

## Refund Policy
Tickets are fully refundable within 24 hours of purchase. After 24 hours, a $75 processing fee applies. Non-refundable tickets can be converted to 12-month travel credit. Refunds take 7-10 business days.

## Flight Change Policy
Changes are free if made more than 7 days before departure. Changes within 2-7 days cost $35. Same-day changes cost $50. No changes are allowed within 2 hours of departure. Fare differences apply.

## Cancellation Policy
Cancellations more than 7 days out: full refund minus $75 fee. Within 7 days: 50% travel credit only. No-shows forfeit the full ticket value.

## Baggage Policy
- Personal item (under seat): free
- 1 carry-on (max 22x14x9 inches, 25 lbs): free
- First checked bag: $35
- Second checked bag: $45
- Additional bags: $75 each
- Oversized bags (over 62 linear inches): $100 fee

## Seat Selection
Standard seats: free during booking. Preferred/extra-legroom seats: $25-$50 extra. Business class upgrades at check-in: $150-$300. Seat changes online up to 24 hours before departure are free.

## Check-in
Online check-in opens 24 hours before departure and closes 1 hour before. Airport check-in closes 45 minutes before domestic flights and 60 minutes before international flights.

## Special Assistance
Passengers requiring wheelchair assistance, medical equipment, or traveling with service animals should call customer support at least 48 hours before departure.
